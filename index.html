VACS - Volleyball Association at Chinese School

Mission/Purpose

Our purpose is to promote the growth and success of Student Volleyball Athletes at Chinese School. The VACS encourage community spirit and aid in the support of special projects and needs of volleyball facility.

Schedule

VACS has same schedule as Chinese School at Solon High School. Opens on Sunday 2:00pm - 5:00pm during Chinese School semesters.

  • 2:00pm - 4:00pm: Open gym for Chinese School community.
  • 4:00pm - 5:00pm: Volleyball class for Chinese School students.
